About me

A designer who thinks like a marketer

Hi! I'm Cindy — a third-year Bachelor of Business (Honours) Marketing student with a genuine passion for visual design. What sets me apart is the way I connect the two: I use market insight, consumer behaviour, and campaign thinking to inform every design decision I make — not just make things look pretty.

To me, great design isn't just aesthetic — it's strategic. Whether I'm crafting a poster or a social graphic, I ask: who is the audience, what do they respond to, and how does this visual serve the message?
